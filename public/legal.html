キャンセルポリシー ご予約のキャンセルにつきましては、以下の通りキャンセル料を申し受けます。
  • 宿泊日14日前〜7日前のキャンセル：宿泊料金の50%
  • 宿泊日7日前〜4日前のキャンセル：宿泊料金の80%
  • 宿泊日3日前〜当日キャンセル：宿泊料金の100%
  • 無連絡不泊：宿泊料金の100%

Accommodation Rates Rates vary by plan. Please refer to the respective plan page for details. All displayed prices are inclusive of consumption tax.
Additional Charges No additional charges beyond the room rate in principle. Additional beverages or other in-house purchases are payable at the front desk or restaurant at time of purchase.
Payment Method & Deadline Accommodation charges are generally prepaid. Payment is processed at the time of booking via our official website or partner booking sites. Any charges beyond the reservation amount are settled on-site.
Service Delivery Accommodation and dining are provided on the dates and according to the plan details reserved. Guests should arrive after the check-in time; dinner and breakfast are served during the times specified in each plan.
Cancellation Policy The following cancellation fees apply:
  • 14–7 days before arrival: 50% of room rate
  • 7–4 days before arrival: 80% of room rate
  • 3 days before arrival to day-of: 100% of room rate
  • No-show: 100% of room rate

Cancellation fees may also apply to dining included with the accommodation. Where cancellation fees apply, the amount will be deducted from any prepaid amount (along with applicable transaction fees) and the remainder refunded.

For accommodation tickets purchased through crowdfunding platforms, the cancellation policy set by the relevant platform operator shall apply.
Refunds Due to the nature of the service, refunds for accommodation and dining already provided may not be possible. Where a refund applies following cancellation, it will be processed in accordance with the cancellation policy above.
